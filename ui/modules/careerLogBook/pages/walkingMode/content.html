In BeamNG.drive, you can not only drive cars, but also walk around outside of them and explore the gameworld.

  • Use [action=unicycle__walkUpDown][showunassigned=false][action=unicycle__walkLeftRight][showunassigned=false] [action=unicycle__walkUp][showunassigned=false][action=unicycle__walkLeft][showunassigned=false][action=unicycle__walkDown][showunassigned=false][action=unicycle__walkRight][showunassigned=false] to walk around.
  • Press [action=unicycle__jump][showunassigned=false] to jump and [action=unicycle__crouchToggle][showunassigned=false] to crouch.
  • Hold [action=unicycle__sprintHold][showunassigned=false] to sprint.
  • Use [action=rotate_camera_vertical][showunassigned=false][action=rotate_camera_horizontal][showunassigned=false] [action=rotate_camera_vt_mouse][showunassigned=false][action=rotate_camera_hz_mouse][showunassigned=false][action=rotate_camera_up][showunassigned=false][action=rotate_camera_left][showunassigned=false][action=rotate_camera_down][showunassigned=false][action=rotate_camera_right][showunassigned=false] to look around.
  • Press [action=toggleWalkingMode][showunassigned=false] to enter or exit vehicles.

Use these controls get in and out of vehicles. Sometimes, it is required to be in walking mode to interact with objects such as doors or computers.
